About Dry Oar Rafting

Our History

The Rapids Rafting has been guiding adventurers through the most exciting rivers since 1998. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region. We believe in safety, fun, and creating unforgettable memories on the water.

Experience the Wild

Our guides are certified professionals with years of experience. Whether you're a beginner or an adrenaline junkie, we have the perfect trip for you.

Why Choose The Rapids?

  • ✔ Scenic routes with wildlife and waterfalls
  • ✔ Safety-focused and family-friendly
  • ✔ Custom tours for groups and events
